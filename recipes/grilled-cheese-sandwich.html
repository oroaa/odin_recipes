Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Description

The classic, the one and only, Grilled Cheese Sandwich. A quick meal for lunch or dinner where you forgot the make something healthier. Made right, it rivals almost any other full meal recipe. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 4 slices of white bread
  • 3 tablespoons butter, divided
  • 2 slices of Cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat a nonstick skillet over medium heat. Generously butter both the pieces of bread and the pan.
  2. Place bread butter-side down in the hot skillet; add 1 slice of cheese. Butter a second slice of bread and place butter-side up on top of cheese.
  3. Cook until lightly browned on one side; then flip and continue cooking until cheese is melted. Repeat with remaining 2 slices of bread, butter, and slice of cheese
